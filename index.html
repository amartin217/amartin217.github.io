About Me

Hi! I’m Allison Martin, a Computer Science Engineering student at the University of Michigan’s College of Engineering. I’m passionate about back-end development—designing efficient systems, working with databases, and building scalable APIs that power intuitive front-end experiences.

I’m especially interested in fintech because it blends technology and problem-solving to make financial systems more accessible, secure, and impactful. During my internship at Charles Schwab, I worked on software that supported the Thinkorswim trading platform, which strengthened my interest in the infrastructure behind modern finance.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy spending time with friends and family and staying active. I’m always eager to learn new technologies and contribute to projects that make a real-world difference.
